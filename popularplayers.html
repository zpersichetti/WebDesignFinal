Most Popular Players

Odell "OBJ" Beckham Jr

Bio: OBJ was born in 1992 in Baton Rouge Louisiana. He followed in his fathers footsteps and went to play football at LSU. He performed very well at LSU winning, in his senior year, the Paul Hornung Award presented to the most versitile player in the FBS. OBJ was drafted by the Giants in the first round at 12 overall. He has broken numerous recieving records already including fastest player to 250 catches and fastest play to 4000 yard. He is known for his circus catches, and often ridiculous celebrations.

Cam Newton

Bio: Cam Newton was born in 1989 in Atlanta. Cam Newton first went to Florida university but did not see any oppurtunity to become the starter there. He then transfered to a junior college and then to Auburn where he lead his team to a National Title and won the Heisman in 2010. He was then take first overall in the 2011 draft by the Panthers. In 2015 Newton lead his team to the Superbowl but came up short against the Broncos. He is know for his ability to run and throw the ball and for how he often towers over defensive players.

Tom Brady

Bio: Tom Brady was born in 1977 in San Mateo California. Brady went to Michigan where he was the backup for most of his freshman and sophmore year. In his junior year he got a chance to start and played very well leading his team to an Orange Bowl victory. Brady was drafted in the sixth round of the 2000 draft by the Patriots and was given the backup position behind Drew Bledsoe. However, in 2001 Drew Bledsoe broke his leg and Tom Brady took over and lead the Patriots to his first superbowl of 5. He is known for being the greatest quarterback of all time and also for a recent scandal where he was caught using deflated footballs in a playoff game to try and grip the ball better.

Justin James "JJ" Watt

Bio: Justin James "JJ" Watt was born in 1989 in Waukesha, Wisconsin. JJ Watt initially went to Central Michigan University where he played tight-end. When his coaches suggested that he play offensive tackle he decided to transfer to Wisconsin and walk on to the football team. In 2010 Watt was awarded the Lott Trophy for his perfomance off the field and his character off the field. He also won All-Big ten first team and was voted team MVP. Watt was selected in the first round by the Texans. Watt is currently considered to be one of the best if not the best defensive linemen in the NFL. He is known for his athletisism and being able to play multiple positions. He is also know for his philanthropy as he is founder of the Justn J. Watt Foundation.

Aaron Rodgers

Bio: Aaron Rodgers was born in 1983 in California. Aaron Rodgers first went to Butte College where he played quarterback. He then transfered to the University of California. In 2004 Aaron Rodgers was a finalist for the Heisman but lost to Matt Leinart. Aaron Rodgers expected to go very early in the 2005 draft but fell all the way to 24th where he was picked by the Packers. With the Packer Aaron Rodgers backed up long time quarterback Brett Farve. When Farve retired in 2008 Rodgers became the starter. In 2011 he lead the Packers to the Superbowl where they defeated the Steelers. He is known for his ability to scramble and throw the ball down field. He is also know for his "discount double check" commercial which became his touchdown celebration.
